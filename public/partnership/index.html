Associations

Are you an association that needs help developing a project ? Let us assist you in bringing your vision to life.

Get started
  • Project management support.
  • Project idea refinement through consultations and brainstorming.
  • Assistance with project planning, timelines, and budgets.
  • Training workshops/resources for project members.

University Professors

Are you a university professor looking for an international project to incorporate into a studio or capstone project? Offer your students an impactful internship (remote or in Morocco), and/or organize a faculty-led trip to Morocco with our support.

Get started
  • Organize faculty-led trips to Morocco.
  • Offer impactful internships (remote or in Morocco).
  • Incorporate international projects into studio or capstone courses.
  • Facilitate cross-cultural collaboration opportunities for students.

Organizations

Are you an organization wanting to organize hands-on service learning trips to Morocco? Let's collaborate to create a meaningful experience for your team.

Get started
  • Tailor service learning experiences to meet your organization's goals
  • Offer cultural immersion opportunities for deeper understanding.
  • Provide logistical support for seamless trip organization.
  • Ensure safety and security measures throughout the trip.
